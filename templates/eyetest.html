Instructions


  1. Please stand meters away from your current screen.
  2. Test your visual acuity with correction (contact lenses or glasses).
  3. Test one eye at a time. Start with the right eye, covering the left one without pressing on it. Then, examine the left eye by doing the opposite.
  4. Read the letters from largest to smallest.
  5. Click the record button to start, and stop whenever you are unable to read any letters.
  6. Click the generate button to view your results.
